# MilestoneFunding

`MilestoneFunding` is a decentralized crowdfunding smart contract that allows creators to launch projects and raise funds in milestones. Investors can vote on each milestone to determine whether funds are released to the creator, ensuring transparency and accountability throughout the project.  

Projects can be interacted with via the **[MileFunds Frontend](https://milefunds.xyz)**.

---

## How to Use
1. Visit **[MileFunds](https://milefunds.xyz)**.  
2. Connect your Ethereum wallet (MetaMask or compatible wallet).  
3. Create projects, fund projects, vote on milestones, and claim funds via the frontend.  

---

## Notes
- Minimum investment: 0.0001 ETH.  
- Milestones are finalized after reaching quorum (70% of total weight voted).  
- Refunds and payouts are calculated proportionally based on investments and project state.
